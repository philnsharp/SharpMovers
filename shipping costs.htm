Mileage Pricing

  • Under 1 mile 1-way $4.99
  • 1-3 miles 1-way $8.99
  • 3-5 miles 1-way $12.99
  • 5-9 miles 1-way $15.99
  • 10+ miles 1-way is charged per 1/2 hour at a rate of $25.99

Additional Weight Costs

  • For items traveling 1-5 miles and over 100lbs an additional $20 will be added for handling
  • For items traveling 1-5 miles and over 200lbs an additional $50 will be added for handling
  • For items traveling 5+ miles and over 100lbs an additional $50 will be added for handling
  • For items traveling 5+ miles and over 200lbs an additional $75 will be added for handling
  • Items exceeding 300lbs will be refused due to potential risk of injury.
